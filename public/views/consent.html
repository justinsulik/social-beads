The HIT you are about to do is an experiment by researchers at Royal Holloway, University of London. You will have to make a decision about the color of beads hidden in an urn, after drawing a few beads from the urn. More detailed HIT instructions will be provided on the next screen.

This task has no anticipated risks. There is a performance-based bonus which will be explained later.

There are also comprehension checks to make sure you understand the instructions. You cannot proceed with the experiment until you pass the comprehension check.

Do not take this HIT on a mobile device (e.g., cell phone or tablet), and do not take it with Internet Explorer.

If you have any questions or concerns about this HIT please contact the principal investigator: Dr Ryan McKay (ryan.mckay@rhul.ac.uk)

If you are not satisfied with the response of the research team or want to talk with someone about your rights as a research participant, you should contact Royal Holloway's research ethics board at ethics@rhul.ac.uk

You are free to decline to participate or to end participation at any time for any reason.
